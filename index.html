LEGACY
Our Guiding Light

The Vision Behind
Rakme Marine.

Rakme Engineering stands as a testament to the vision of our late founder, whose passion for marine engineering laid the keel for everything we build today.

Starting with a modest workshop in 1995, he believed that "Quality at sea is not a luxury, it is a lifeline." His insistence on precision manufacturing earned us our first Ministry of Defence contracts.

Today, we continue to navigate by his compass—honoring his legacy by maintaining the rigorous standards he established over three decades ago.
